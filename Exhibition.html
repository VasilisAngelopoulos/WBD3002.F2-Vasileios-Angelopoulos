At Zappeion Halls a new exhibition on the relationship of art and marble

  At the Zappeion Hall in Athens one of the most important natural stone and technology fairs worldwide takes place. In a country with a high percentage of marble reserves and a high degree of diversity, marble unites thousands of trade visitors, such as contractors, architects, project managers, advisory services and more professional visitors under one roof. The exhibitors show the natural stones in all shapes and facets, among others as blocks, slabs and tiles. They present different processing techniques and machines emphasizing the importance of marble and natural stones for the national economy. The exhibition centre is 34 km away from International Airport. The exhibition program is aimed at presenting in one place and time market novelties, the latest technologies and finished stone products. During the three days of the fair, specialists and experts from the stone industry will talk about the use of stone in construction, decoration, current achievements and trends in the field of design. We have also prepared a zone where you will be able to see machines in motion using raw materials tools and accessories. The strategic location in the immediate vicinity of the motorways makes the journey comfortable and stress-free. The exhibition centre is also easily accessible by public transport. Within the hosted buyer program free accommodation and transfer between airport, hotel and exhibition centre is granted.
